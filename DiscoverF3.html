Tinolang Manok

Tinolang Manok is a traditional Filipino chicken soup dish characterized by its clear broth infused with ginger and lemongrass, accompanied by tender chicken pieces, green papaya or chayote, and leafy greens like spinach or malunggay leaves. It's a comforting and nourishing dish commonly served with steamed rice, enjoyed across the Philippines for its refreshing flavors and heartwarming appeal.
