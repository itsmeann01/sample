Adobong Manok

Adobong Manok is a classic Filipino dish featuring tender chicken pieces marinated and simmered in a flavorful blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices. The dish is known for its rich and savory taste with a hint of tanginess from the vinegar. It's often served with steamed rice and enjoyed as a hearty and comforting meal in Filipino households.
